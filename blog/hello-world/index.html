I couldn’t find a better way to start something with the classic “hello world,” however, this is not going to be a blog related to only coding (there are going to be some posts about it but is not the main topic). The idea of this blog is to communicate, share knowledge and experiences of my everyday interaction with technology. I’m lucky and very grateful to do what I love as a daily job. So I’m continually doing security-related stuff as part of my work and always try to keep updated or to learn and try different things during my free time in my home lab.

I know that start a blog may be the easy part; the hardest part is going to be to continually add content as it requires dedication and time, which I will try to find.

What topics would I like to cover? on top of my mind (but not limited to):

  • Virtualization
  • Network Security
  • Automation
  • Scripting
  • Python and Golang
  • Fortinet Solutions (where I work hehe)
  • Home Lab (I like to play and build my home lab systems)
  • SIEM - Log Management - Data Analytics
  • Vulnerability Research
  • Pentesting
  • And any other topic that catches my attention. :)
